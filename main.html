Pollution

Pollution is the act or introduction of contaminating a certain space using objects/substances. Each year, there are 268,000,000 pounds of trash being thrown out across families in th e US alone. Of that 268 million, a whopping 44% of that is either not disposed of, or worse, not disposed correctly. Where does all this trash go then, well, into the ocean. Ocean pollution is becoming a common thing in todays world, with major rivers like India's Ganges River being almost unusable due to all the toxic substances inside the water. Every day, Garbage kills 273 ocean mammals. That is not even citing the dying coral reefs, which lose their once beautiful all because of their pollutants. But, the ocean is not defenseless, many people around the world organize communities dedicated to make the oceans, and the world itself a better place by cleaning up waste around the world. Greenhouse Gases

"Climate change describes a change in the average conditions over a long period of time. Scientists agree that the Earth has been getting warmer over the last century due to human activities that require burning fossil fuels (oil, gas, and coal). Burning fossil fuels releases carbon dioxide. The carbon dioxide becomes trapped in the atmosphere (greenhouse gases) and increases the planet's temperature. As the Earth's climate continues to warm the results can have big impacts on the environment (From ecology.wa.gov.)" We can lower these gases by switching to renewable energy, which is not only healthy for the environment, but also for your wallet, as some states (Like NJ) will PAY you to switch to these energy sources.
